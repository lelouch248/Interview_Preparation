# Problem — Data Quality Control Tower

## Scenario

Engineers and product owners need one place to understand freshness, completeness, failures, schema changes and ownership.

## Questions to solve

- What systems provide metadata and health signals?
- What quality dimensions are measured?
- How are rules configured and versioned?
- How are incidents grouped to avoid alert fatigue?
- How are lineage and business impact represented?
- Which metrics are for engineers vs product owners?
- What should an AI agent explain, and what must remain deterministic?
