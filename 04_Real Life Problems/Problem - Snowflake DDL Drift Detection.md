# Problem — Snowflake DDL Drift Detection

## Scenario

Compare production with another Snowflake environment and classify objects as missing, changed or cosmetically different.

## Questions to solve

- Which object types and schemas are in scope?
- How will you retrieve and normalise DDL?
- Which differences are safe, risky or breaking?
- How will environment-specific names be tokenised?
- How will false positives be reviewed?
- What evidence and reports are generated?
- What remediation can be automated safely?
- What requires human approval?
