# Behavioral Interviews & Project Storytelling

**Priority:** Core Interview Skill

## Outcome

- Explain your work with ownership, impact and technical depth.
- Answer behavioral questions without sounding memorized.
- Build strong stories for your actual projects.

## Study checklist

### Personal Narrative

- [ ] Tell me about yourself
- [ ] Why are you changing roles?
- [ ] Why this company and role?
- [ ] Your strongest technical area
- [ ] A growth area and what you are doing about it

### STAR Story Bank

- [ ] Difficult bug or incident
- [ ] Performance or cost optimization
- [ ] Automation that removed manual work
- [ ] Ambiguous requirement
- [ ] Conflict or disagreement
- [ ] Failure and recovery
- [ ] Leadership without authority
- [ ] Learning a new technology quickly

### Project Deep Dives

- [ ] Problem and business context
- [ ] Scale and constraints
- [ ] Architecture and your contribution
- [ ] Hardest technical decision
- [ ] Testing, monitoring and security
- [ ] Measured impact
- [ ] What you would redesign now

- [ ] Questions to ask the interviewer
- [ ] Compensation and notice-period discussion basics
