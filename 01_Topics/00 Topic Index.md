## How to mark a topic complete

A topic file is complete only when you can:

- Explain the major concepts without reading.
- Solve representative interview questions.
- Apply the concepts to a realistic engineering scenario.
- Discuss trade-offs, failure modes, security and cost where relevant.
- Revisit and pass a later revision or mock round.
